Description

This track provides Prdm9 DNA binding sites for the T2T-CHM13 genome assembly (Nurk et al., 2022) by the Telomere-to-Telomere consortium.

Methods

  • We applied MOTIFENCE (v0.1.1, commit fb1ebc0) to find DNA sequences matching the canonical 13-mer motif CCNCCNTNNCCNC (Myers et al., 2008):

    motifence --fasta chm13v2.0.fa --motif CCNCCNTNNCCNC | bedtools sort | bgzip -@ 12 -l 9 > chm13v2.0.CCNCCNTNNCCNC.bed.gz
